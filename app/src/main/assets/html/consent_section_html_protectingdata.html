Your privacy is important to us.

The data collected through the app will be encrypted on the phone.

We will separate your account information (name, email, contact information, etc.) from the study data (the responses to surveys, the mole pictures and the mole measurements). The metadata will consist of your responses to survey and mole measurements only. No mole pictures will be included in the metadata.

We will use a random code instead of your name on all study data. This code cannot be used to directly re-identify you. Information about the code will be kept in a separate secure system. Only key personnel from the study team may connect your study data to your name and account information.
