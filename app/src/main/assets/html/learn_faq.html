Does this app tell me if I have melanoma?

This app is designed for self-tracking to help you monitor the size, shape, and color of your moles. This app does not make any claim to detect, diagnose, or treat any skin condition. Any data stored within the app is for personal use only. We encourage you to share your photos and measurements with a licensed professional and refer to their professional opinion.
